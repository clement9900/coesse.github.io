Coesse

One World. One Breath.

Coesse is a volunteer-run, non-profit initiative founded by anonymous individuals. We believe that the protection of the Earth and the defense of human dignity begin with individual awareness and everyday action.

Mission

To mobilize global volunteers to protect the planet, defend human rights, and cultivate a culture of coexistence for present and future generations.

Vision

A world where humanity and nature coexist in harmony, every life is respected, and individuals across cultures and borders unite to shape a sustainable and just future.

Core Belief

We see the Earth as a living system. All forms of life are interconnected, worthy of protection, and deserving of fundamental rights.
